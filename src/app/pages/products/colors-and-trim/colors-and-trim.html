How Our Process Works

At New Bath Expo, we use an innovative process to create high-quality, low-maintenance shower wall materials.

  • After combing through hundreds of options, we select aesthetically pleasing stone slabs made from marble, granite, travertine, and more.
  • Using advanced technology, we scan these slabs at high resolutions to capture their intricate details, patterns, and colors.
  • These patterns are included as part of our multi-layered composite material in order to authentically replicate the look of real stone.

Whether you're remodeling, building from the ground up, working in a multi-family property, or outfitting a hospitality setting, New Bath Expo's high-quality materials and customizable options allow you to create the perfect space with ease.
